The Proposal

MegaTough will be a blog and community responding to a failed understanding of what it means to be human and to live a good life.

Is there such a thing in modernity as a life well lived? What happened to pledging one's life toward the pursuit of wisdom and virtue? Is our culture cultivating within us all we hoped it would?

The premise of MegaTough will be that our culture, in trying to find itself, may very well have lost its way. We forgot there is nothing new under the sun. And if we are now to recover lost truth, we need to go back, remember, and yield our modern myopic understanding to a perspective of greater breadth.
